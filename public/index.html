Privacy is Not a Feature, It's Our Promise.

Cradly works with a "Local-First" architecture. Your data lives only on your device, not in the cloud. This way, you can access your data anytime, even without internet.

All data on your device is encrypted at a military level with AES-256 (SQLCipher) technology. Even we can never access your data.

Important Note: Cradly is not a medical device or health application. It is a professional note-taking and tracking assistant.
